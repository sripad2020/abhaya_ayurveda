Our Philosophy

The Science of Life — Ayurveda

Derived from the Sanskrit words Āyus (life) and Veda (knowledge), Ayurveda is the world's oldest holistic healing system. AyuSutra channels this living tradition — combining classical texts like Charaka Samhita and Sushruta Samhita with evidence-informed modern care.

We believe every body is unique. Your Prakruti — your constitutional type — determines how disease manifests and how healing unfolds. Our protocols are never generic; they are as individual as you.
